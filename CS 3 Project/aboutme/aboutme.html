My Story

My name is Mendrado Canusa. This website was made for my Computer Science 3 project. Although this may just be a project to some, but Pearth can be so much more. The health of our planet is deteriorating. Every day we inch closer to its inevitable end. If no one was to take responsibility in taking initiative to help our planet, let it be me. This conviction inspired me to create Pearth. A healthy environment that aims to spread and take action on the current environmental and political problems arroung the world.
